Using the Website

On the Margins of History is an online platform designed to support the study of the Latin, Old English and runic marginalia in Cambridge, Corpus Christi College MS 41. The following guidelines are intended to help users navigate the site and interact successfully with CCCC41 and its extensive range of marginalia

Video Tutorial

The video tutorial below demonstrates how users can navigate the site and access the website's various features.

Instructions for Loading the Mirador Viewer

Step 1:
Navigate to the Explore the Manuscript page of the website. Scroll down to the instructions under the Mirador viewer.
Step 2:
Right click on the link provided for The Parker Library 2.0 Manifest of CCCC41 and open in a new tab.
Step 3:
Click and hold on the icon in the bottom right corner underneath the Parker Library's Mirador viewer.
Step 4:
Drag the icon to the tab containing this webpage (Exploring the Manuscript).
Step 5:
Drop the icon into the Mirador viewer instance on this page. The Mirador viewer will prompt you to do this once you have dragged the icon within the Mirador frame.
Step 6:
Explore the Manuscript!
